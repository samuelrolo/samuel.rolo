CV Analyser - Data Processing

Data Collected

When you use our CV Analyser service, we collect and process:

  • CV File: Document uploaded in PDF or DOCX format
  • Personal CV Data: Name, contact, professional experience, academic background, skills
  • Contact Data: Email and mobile phone for report delivery (paid service only)

Purpose of Processing

Data is processed exclusively for:

  • Automatic analysis of the CV's structure and content
  • Generation of a personalized report with recommendations
  • Sending the report to the provided email (paid service)

Legal Basis

The processing is based on your explicit consent, given at the moment of uploading the CV.

Storage and Retention

  • Free Analysis: The CV is processed locally in your browser and is not sent to our servers. No data is stored.
  • Paid Report: The CV and analysis data are temporarily stored (base64) in Google Cloud Datastore only during the payment process and report delivery. Data is automatically deleted after 30 days.

Data Sharing

Your data may be shared with:

  • Google Cloud Platform: Secure hosting and processing (paid service)
  • Brevo (Sendinblue): Sending transactional emails with report
  • Ifthenpay: MB WAY payment processing
  • Google AI (Gemini): CV content analysis (anonymized data)

Security

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • SSL/TLS encryption on all communications
  • Secure storage in base64
  • Restricted access to data by authorized personnel
  • Automatic deletion after retention period

Data Subject Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify incorrect data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • File a complaint with CNPD (National Data Protection Commission)

To exercise your rights, contact: geral@share2inspire.pt

Share2Inspire Job Saver — Chrome Extension

Service Description

The Share2Inspire Job Saver is a free extension for the Google Chrome browser that allows users to detect and save job offers from supported platforms (LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor, Adzuna) directly to their Share2Inspire member area.

Data Collected

When you use the Job Saver extension, the following data is collected from the job offer page you are viewing:

  • Job Title: The job offer title
  • Company Name: The name of the hiring company
  • Location: The job's location as presented in the ad
  • Source URL: The address of the job offer page
  • Platform: The source platform (LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor, or Adzuna)

The extension does not collect any personal data, browsing history, cookies, credentials, or any information beyond the job offer details described above.

Purpose of Processing

Data is processed exclusively to:

  • Display the detected job details in the extension’s sidebar panel
  • Save job offers in the user's Share2Inspire member area for later reference
  • Export saved jobs in CSV format

Storage and Retention

  • Local Storage: Saved jobs are stored locally in the browser via chrome.storage.local. This data remains on the user’s device and is not transmitted to external servers, except when the user is authenticated in their Share2Inspire account.
  • Cloud Storage: When authenticated, saved jobs are synchronized with the user’s Share2Inspire member area via Supabase (secure cloud database). Data is retained as long as the user’s account remains active.

Data Sharing

Data for jobs saved through the extension is not shared with third parties. Data is only transmitted to:

  • Supabase: Secure cloud database to store saved jobs in the user’s member area (only when authenticated)

Permissions

The extension requires the following browser permissions:

  • activeTab: To detect job offer pages when the user clicks the extension icon
  • storage: To locally save job offers on the user’s device
  • scripting: To extract job details (title, company, location) from supported job platforms
  • sidePanel: To display the list of saved jobs in a browser sidebar panel
  • Host Permissions: Limited to linkedin.com, indeed.com, glassdoor.com, and adzuna.com to detect and extract job offer information

Security

We implement the following measures to protect your data:

  • All communications with the cloud database use SSL/TLS encryption
  • The extension does not execute remote code
  • No data is collected or transmitted when the extension is not actively used
  • The extension only accesses the content of pages on the four supported job platforms

To exercise your data rights or request deletion of saved jobs, contact: geral@share2inspire.pt
