Executive Summary

This study, conducted by Share2Inspire based on an analysis of 500 representative profiles from the Portuguese market, reveals a concerning reality: the vast majority of professionals in Portugal are not taking advantage of LinkedIn's potential. With an average optimisation score of just 4.00 out of 10, the data indicates that 74% of profiles have a visibility rated as "Low" for the platform's algorithms.

The Anatomy of a LinkedIn Profile in Portugal

The distribution of optimisation scores is strongly skewed, with the vast majority of profiles concentrated in the lower end of the scale. Only 0.6% of the professionals analysed achieve a score above 7.

Performance by Sector

Sales and Commercial lead with 4.33/10, possibly due to greater awareness of the importance of networking. Education and Training present the lowest score (3.60/10).

Performance by Age Group

Professionals aged 31–35 have the most optimised profiles. The youngest (18–24) and most senior (51+) show lower scores.

Key Indicators

Algorithm Visibility

Only 26% of profiles achieve "Medium" visibility, with the remaining 74% considered to have "Low" visibility. No profile in the sample reached "High" visibility.

Conclusion

The Portuguese digital ecosystem on LinkedIn is far from reaching maturity. Most professionals adopt a passive stance, resulting in invisible profiles and missed opportunities. The good news is that the barrier to standing out is low.
