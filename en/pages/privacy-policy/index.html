CV Analyser - Data Processing

Service Description

The CV Analyser is a digital product that uses artificial intelligence to analyse your CV and generate a personalised report with recommendations. The paid report is priced at €9.99. A free basic analysis is also available.

Data Collected

When you use our CV Analyser service, we collect and process:

  • CV File: Document uploaded in PDF or DOCX format
  • Personal Data from CV: Name, contact information, professional experience, education, skills
  • Contact Data: Email and phone number for report delivery (paid service only)

Purpose of Processing

The data is processed exclusively for:

  • Automatic analysis of the CV's structure and content
  • Generation of a personalised report with recommendations
  • Sending the report to the provided email (paid service)

Legal Basis

Processing is based on your explicit consent, provided at the time of CV upload.

Storage and Retention

  • Free Analysis: The CV is processed locally in your browser and is not sent to our servers. No data is stored.
  • Paid Report: The CV and analysis data are temporarily stored (base64) in Google Cloud Datastore only during the payment and report delivery process. The data is automatically deleted after 30 days.

Data Sharing

Your data may be shared with:

  • Google Cloud Platform: Secure hosting and processing (paid service)
  • Brevo (Sendinblue): Sending transactional emails with the report
  • Google AI (Gemini): CV content analysis (anonymised data)
  • PayPal / Stripe: Payment processing (see Payments section)

Security

We implement technical and organisational measures to protect your data:

  • SSL/TLS encryption in all communications
  • Secure storage in base64
  • Restricted access to data by authorised personnel
  • Automatic deletion after the retention period

Data Subject Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify incorrect data
  • Request the deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • File a complaint with the CNPD (National Data Protection Commission)

To exercise your rights, contact: geral@share2inspire.pt

Share2Inspire Job Saver — Chrome Extension

Service Description

The Share2Inspire Job Saver is a free Chrome browser extension that allows users to detect and save job postings from supported platforms (LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor, Adzuna) directly into their Share2Inspire member area.

Data Collected

When you use the Job Saver extension, the following data is collected from the job listing page you are viewing:

  • Job Title: The title of the job posting
  • Company Name: The name of the hiring company
  • Location: The job location as displayed on the listing
  • Source URL: The URL of the job posting page
  • Platform: The source platform (LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor, or Adzuna)

The extension does not collect any personal data, browsing history, cookies, credentials, or any information beyond the job listing details described above.

Purpose of Processing

The data is processed exclusively for:

  • Displaying detected job details in the extension’s side panel
  • Saving job postings to the user’s Share2Inspire member area for later reference
  • Exporting saved jobs to CSV format

Storage and Retention

  • Local Storage: Saved jobs are stored locally in the browser using chrome.storage.local. This data remains on the user’s device and is not transmitted to external servers unless the user is logged in to their Share2Inspire account.
  • Cloud Storage: When logged in, saved jobs are synchronised with the user’s Share2Inspire member area via Supabase (secure cloud database). Data is retained as long as the user’s account is active.

Data Sharing

Job data saved through the extension is not shared with any third parties. The data is only transmitted to:

  • Supabase: Secure cloud database for storing saved jobs in the user’s member area (only when logged in)

Permissions

The extension requires the following browser permissions:

  • activeTab: To detect job listing pages when the user clicks the extension icon
  • storage: To save job postings locally on the user’s device
  • scripting: To extract job details (title, company, location) from supported job platforms
  • sidePanel: To display the saved jobs list in a browser side panel
  • Host permissions: Limited to linkedin.com, indeed.com, glassdoor.com, and adzuna.com to detect and extract job posting information

Security

We implement the following measures to protect your data:

  • All communications with the cloud database use SSL/TLS encryption
  • The extension does not execute remote code
  • No data is collected or transmitted when the extension is not actively being used
  • The extension only accesses page content on the four supported job platforms

To exercise your data rights or request deletion of saved jobs, contact: geral@share2inspire.pt

Third-Party Cookies

In some special cases, we also use cookies provided by trusted third parties:
