Transparency and Security

Privacy Policy

Learn how we handle and protect your personal data.

Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. It is Share2Inspire's policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect on the Share2Inspire website.

We only ask for personal information when we truly need it to provide you with a service. We do so by fair and lawful means, with your knowledge and consent. We also let you know why we are collecting it and how it will be used.

Data Retention and Protection

We only retain collected information for as long as necessary to provide the requested service. When we store data, we protect it within commercially acceptable means to prevent loss and theft, as well as unauthorized access, disclosure, copying, use, or modification.

Information Sharing

We do not share personally identifying information publicly or with third parties, except when required by law.

External Links

Our website may link to external sites that are not operated by us. Please be aware that we have no control over the content and practices of these sites and cannot accept responsibility or liability for their respective privacy policies.

Your Rights

You are free to refuse our request for your personal information, with the understanding that we may be unable to provide you with some of your desired services.

Your continued use of our website will be regarded as acceptance of our practices around privacy and personal information. If you have any questions about how we handle user data and personal information, feel free to contact us.

CV Analyser - Data Processing

Service Description

The CV Analyser is a digital product that uses artificial intelligence to analyse your CV and generate a personalised report with recommendations. The paid report is priced at $5. A free basic analysis is also available.

Data Collected

When you use our CV Analyser service, we collect and process:

  • CV File: Document uploaded in PDF or DOCX format
  • Personal Data from CV: Name, contact information, professional experience, education, skills
  • Contact Data: Email and phone number for report delivery (paid service only)

Purpose of Processing

The data is processed exclusively for:

  • Automatic analysis of the CV's structure and content
  • Generation of a personalised report with recommendations
  • Sending the report to the provided email (paid service)

Legal Basis

Processing is based on your explicit consent, provided at the time of CV upload.

Storage and Retention

  • Free Analysis: The CV is processed locally in your browser and is not sent to our servers. No data is stored.
  • Paid Report: The CV and analysis data are temporarily stored (base64) in Google Cloud Datastore only during the payment and report delivery process. The data is automatically deleted after 30 days.

Data Sharing

Your data may be shared with:

  • Google Cloud Platform: Secure hosting and processing (paid service)
  • Brevo (Sendinblue): Sending transactional emails with the report
  • Google AI (Gemini): CV content analysis (anonymised data)
  • PayPal / Stripe: Payment processing (see Payments section)

Security

We implement technical and organisational measures to protect your data:

  • SSL/TLS encryption in all communications
  • Secure storage in base64
  • Restricted access to data by authorised personnel
  • Automatic deletion after the retention period

Data Subject Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify incorrect data
  • Request the deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • File a complaint with the CNPD (National Data Protection Commission)

To exercise your rights, contact: srshare2inspire@gmail.com

Career Path - Data Processing

Service Description

The Career Path is a digital product that generates a personalised career plan based on the professional data provided by the user. The service is priced at $10.

Data Collected

When you use Career Path, we collect and process:

  • Professional Data: Professional experience, skills, and career goals provided through the form
  • Contact Data: Email for delivery of the generated career plan

Purpose of Processing

The data is processed exclusively for:

  • Generation of the personalised career plan using artificial intelligence
  • Sending the career plan to the provided email

Legal Basis

Processing is based on your explicit consent, provided at the time of completing the form and making the payment.

Storage and Retention

The professional data and the generated career plan are stored temporarily during the payment and delivery process. The data is automatically deleted after 30 days.

Data Sharing

Your data may be shared with:

  • Google AI (Gemini): Generation of the personalised career plan
  • Brevo (Sendinblue): Sending the career plan by email
  • PayPal / Stripe: Payment processing (see Payments section)

Payments - Data Processing

Accepted Payment Methods

The Share2Inspire website accepts the following payment methods:

  • PayPal: Secure payment through the PayPal platform
  • Stripe: Payment by credit or debit card (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)

How We Process Payments

Share2Inspire does not store or have access to credit or debit card data. All payment data is processed directly and securely by our payment partners:

  • PayPal: PayPal payments are processed entirely by the PayPal platform, subject to their Privacy Policy.
  • Stripe: Card payments are processed entirely by Stripe, Inc., subject to their Privacy Policy. Stripe is PCI DSS Level 1 certified, the highest security standard for payment processing.

Billing Data

For billing and transaction record purposes, we may store:

  • Transaction reference (payment ID)
  • Transaction amount and date
  • Email associated with the payment

This data is retained for the legally required period for tax and accounting purposes.

Cookie Policy

What are cookies?

As is common practice with almost all professional websites, this site uses cookies, which are tiny files downloaded to your computer, to improve your experience. This page describes what information they gather, how we use it, and why we sometimes need to store these cookies.

How we use cookies

We use cookies for a variety of reasons detailed below. Unfortunately, in most cases, there are no industry-standard options for disabling cookies without completely disabling the functionality and features they add to this site. It is recommended that you leave on all cookies if you are not sure whether you need them or not.

Disabling cookies

You can prevent the setting of cookies by adjusting the settings on your browser (see your browser's Help for how to do this). Be aware that disabling cookies will affect the functionality of this and many other websites that you visit.

Cookies we set

  • Account-related cookies: If you create an account with us, we will use cookies for the management of the signup process and general administration.
  • Login-related cookies: We use cookies when you are logged in so that we can remember this action.
  • Form-related cookies: When you submit data through a form, cookies may be set to remember user details.
  • Preference cookies: To provide you with a great experience, we provide the functionality to set your preferences.

Third-Party Cookies

In some special cases, we also use cookies provided by trusted third parties:

  • Google Analytics: This site uses Google Analytics to help us understand how you use the site and how we can improve your experience.
