About

Humanizing technology. Making organizational innovation sustainable. Leading with intention.

I'm Samuel Rolo, a Human Resources and organisational transformation professional with over 14 years of experience in international consulting, having worked on digital and cultural transformation projects in major organisations.

I don't believe in organizational transformation without human transformation. Throughout this journey in global Human Resources and digital transformation contexts, I've learned that technology accelerates processes, but it's people who determine its impact.

I have worked at the intersection of corporate strategy, global HR, and cultural change. Today, my focus is clear: to humanize technology and make organizational innovation sustainable.

"The future of work will not be defined solely by artificial intelligence, automation, or operational efficiency. It will be defined by those who can integrate critical thinking, empathy, and strategic vision into an increasingly algorithmic world."

Samuel Rolo is a Portuguese Human Resources professional specializing in Digital HR, Process Excellence, and Organizational Transformation, with an approach centered on the intersection of technology and humanity.

Throughout his career, he has built a professional identity based on three axes: operational rigor, strategic thinking, and ethical awareness in the use of technology applied to people.

Education and Academic Background

He studied at ISCTE – University Institute of Lisbon and ISCAL – Lisbon Institute of Accounting and Administration, where he stood out for his academic performance, receiving a merit award.

During his studies, he joined initiatives such as ISCTE Junior Consulting, which from an early stage reinforced the link between theory, practice, and organizational impact.

Professional Career

He began his career in consulting, working at Deloitte, where he progressed to Senior Manager. He also had a stint at EY, later returning to Deloitte to consolidate his experience in complex transformation projects, especially in the financial industry.

In 2025, he took on the role of Digital HR Process Manager at AstraZeneca, with responsibility for building and consolidating an HR Process Excellence function focused on:

  • Harmonization of end-to-end global processes
  • Structured governance of digital initiatives
  • Data quality in platforms like Workday
  • Prioritization of technological demand in coordination with HR IT and Global Business Services
  • Progressive integration of automation and artificial intelligence in HR

His focus is to ensure that technology does not replace human judgment but enhances the strategic capacity of teams.

Areas of Expertise

Samuel positions himself at the human-technological frontier, arguing that the real turning point is not in the tool, but in the cultural maturity of the organization.

His core areas include:

  • HR process transformation
  • Artificial Intelligence applied to People and Culture
  • Digital ethics and AI governance
  • Data-driven employee experience
  • Algorithmic literacy and employability

He has a particular interest in the impact of automated systems on employability, namely the functioning of ATS and how they influence recruitment decisions.

Intellectual Production

He is the author of five books, including:

  • Enquanto houver vento há sentimento (As long as there is wind, there is feeling)
  • Gestão da Mudança: Estratégias para Liderança e Transformação Organizacional (Change Management: Strategies for Leadership and Organizational Transformation)
  • The 5 Minute Reset: How Busy People Transform Their Lives Without Changing Their Schedule

His books combine personal reflection, applied psychology, and practical frameworks, with a direct and structured style.

Digital Presence and Recognition

Samuel has built a consistent presence on LinkedIn, where he is recognized as a Top Voice in the areas of Human Resources and Consulting, being among the most influential creators in Portugal in the categories of productivity, personal growth, and innovation.

He is the founder of the Share2Inspire platform, dedicated to strategic career development. Through this initiative, he created solutions such as the CV Analyzer, which helps professionals understand how their resumes are interpreted by automated systems.

He regularly publishes newsletters and articles on:

  • AI at work
  • Organizational culture
  • Future of careers
  • Technological responsibility

Leadership Philosophy

"Sustainable transformation does not happen by technological imposition; it happens when there is alignment between purpose, process, and people."

He advocates a leadership that combines high standards and empathy, strategic clarity, and social awareness. He believes that digital maturity is not measured by the number of tools implemented, but by the quality of the decisions that these tools enable.

Personal Dimension

Born in Lisbon in 1987, he lives in Mafra. He values continuous development, discipline, detail, and coherence between discourse and practice.

His communication reflects a balance between critical thinking and vulnerability, also addressing themes such as self-esteem, identity, overcoming challenges, and building meaning.
