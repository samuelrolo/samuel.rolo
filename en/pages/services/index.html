Frequently asked questions

Is CV Analyser really free?

Yes. The initial analysis with maturity score and key recommendations is 100% free. If you want the full report with 15+ detailed recommendations and ATS rate, it costs €9.99.

What's the difference between Career Path and Career Intelligence?

Career Path gives you a personalised roadmap with your ideal next step. Career Intelligence goes further: it compares multiple paths, analyses trade-offs and gives you a justified recommendation. If you already know the direction, Career Path is enough. If you're torn between options, Career Intelligence is for you.

How long does it take to get results?

CV Analyser and LinkedIn Roaster are instant — you get results in seconds. Career Path and Career Intelligence are generated in under 5 minutes after payment.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. Your data is processed securely and never shared with third parties. We comply with GDPR and you can request deletion of your data at any time.

I've tried career tools before and they didn't work.

Most tools give generic advice. Our analyses are personalised to your profile, experience and market. We don't tell you "improve your CV" — we tell you exactly what, why and how. And you start free, with zero risk.
