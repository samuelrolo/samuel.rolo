# Share2Inspire — Job Saver Extension

## Installation & Usage Guide

The **Job Saver** extension lets you automatically save job listings while browsing LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor and Adzuna, directly to your Share2Inspire member area.

---

### Step 1 — Download & Install

1. Log in to your **member area** at [share2inspire.pt](https://share2inspire.pt) and go to the **Jobs** tab.
2. Click **Download ZIP** to get the extension file.
3. Extract the ZIP file to a folder on your computer (e.g. Desktop).
4. In Chrome, open **chrome://extensions**, enable **Developer mode** (top right corner) and click **Load unpacked**.
5. Select the extracted folder — the extension is now installed and its icon appears in the Chrome toolbar.

---

### Step 2 — Log In

1. Click the extension icon in the Chrome toolbar to open the **side panel**.
2. Enter your Share2Inspire **email** and **password**.
3. Click **Log In** — you are now authenticated and ready to save jobs.

---

### Step 3 — Save Jobs

1. Browse to a job listing on **LinkedIn**, **Indeed**, **Glassdoor** or **Adzuna**.
2. The extension automatically detects the job and fills in the fields (title, company, location, salary and source).
3. Add notes if you wish and click **Save Job**.

---

### Step 4 — Manage in the Dashboard

1. Go to your **member area** at [share2inspire.pt](https://share2inspire.pt) and open the **Jobs** tab.
2. View all your saved jobs, organised by status: **Saved**, **To Apply**, **Applied**, **Interview** and **Offer**.
3. Update the status of each job as you progress through the recruitment process.

---

> **Supported platforms:** LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor and Adzuna (19 regional domains).
