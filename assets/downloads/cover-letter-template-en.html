Structural Guide

Cover Letter That Generates Interviews

01 — The Hook: Avoid "I am writing to apply for the position of…". Start with something that shows you know the company or its current challenge.
I have been closely following the transformation that [Company Name] has been leading in the [Sector] market, especially following the recent announcement about [News/Project]. It is with this focus on innovation that I believe I can make a meaningful contribution.
02 — The Argument (Impact): Show measurable results. Don't list tasks — tell a concrete win with numbers.
In my last role, I achieved a 38% reduction in operational time, resulting in €280k in savings in the first year. My focus is on transforming complex processes into efficient, measurable outcomes.
03 — Alignment: Show that your motivation is specific to this company — not a copy-paste for every vacancy.
Your culture of [Company Value] resonates with the way I work, where collaboration and agility are priorities.
04 — Call to Action: End with initiative. Propose a clear, professional next step.
I am available for a brief conversation to explore how these skills can support your team's goals. Thank you for your time and consideration.
